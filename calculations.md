## Calculations

**Days Between Launch and Splashdown**

The launch date was July 16, 1969 and the splashdown date was July 24, 1969. The interval is **8 days**.

**Average Translunar Speed**

Distance to the Moon ≈ 384,400 km.
Translunar coast duration ≈ 73 hours.
Average speed = 384,400 km / 73 h ≈ **5,266 km/h**.
